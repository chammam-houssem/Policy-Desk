Methodology & sources ·

Confidence levels

Each plant is tagged high, medium, or low confidence based on the independence and recency of its sources. High requires at least two independent post-2022 references (one institutional). Medium reflects a single institutional source or corroborated press reporting. Low indicates a single non-institutional source.

Draft / estimated fields

Fields labelled draft / estimated are values that have been entered to keep the schema complete but have not yet been verified against a primary source. Capacity figures are reported as published design capacity; actual production may differ.

Beneficiaries

Beneficiary counts are plant-specific where reported. Programme-level beneficiary figures (for example, regional water security programmes) are noted in the source field but not aggregated into plant-level counts.

Data vintage

Source documents are dated where available. The dataset itself is current as of May 2026; subsequent announcements are not yet incorporated.
