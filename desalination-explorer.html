Tunisia Desalination Explorer

Tunisia's water security is increasingly being shaped by energy-intensive coastal desalination infrastructure. This explorer maps public and announced desalination projects so capacity, served regions, and the energy and brine implications can be read together. Figures are drawn from public source notes; unknowns are marked rather than guessed.
