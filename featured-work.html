When Water Is an Energy Question: Energy Transition in Water-Scarce Tunisia

Published in The Bulletin • 2024

This article highlights how Tunisia's energy transition intersects with its water crisis, emphasizing that desalination powered by renewable energy offers a viable path forward. It argues for urgent legal and policy reforms to reflect the true value of water, unlock investment in clean energy, and support integrated planning that ensures long-term sustainability in both sectors.

Adjusting the Dinar for Inflation

Published in Marsad Budget • 2023

This article introduces an interactive inflation adjustment calculator to help Tunisians understand the real value of money over time. By contextualizing prices, wages, and budgets historically, the tool promotes financial literacy and transparency in public debates on inflation and spending.
