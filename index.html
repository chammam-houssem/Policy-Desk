Houssem Chammam

Public Policy Analyst specializing in Energy, Finance, and Sustainable Development

I'm a public policy analyst specializing in public finance, energy, and sustainable development. I've led research and advocacy projects in Tunisia, the US, and across the Mediterranean, working to advance the energy transition in complex, resource-constrained settings.

My expertise spans cross-sectoral policy, stakeholder engagement, data analysis, and communicating policy to diverse audiences. I am passionate about building practical solutions for countries facing water scarcity, debt, and political fragmentation. Currently, my work focuses on the intersection of water and energy policy in North Africa and the Mediterranean.

I regularly contribute to policy research and advocacy initiatives, having worked with organizations such as Al Bawsala, the North African Policy Initiative, and GIGA. I hold a Master of Public Policy from the Willy Brandt School in Germany and a Bachelor's in Political Science from Carleton College in the USA.
